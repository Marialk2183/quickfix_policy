Privacy Policy for QuickFix

Last updated: 8 May 2026

QuickFix ("we", "our", or "the App") is operated by Maria Lokhandwala. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use QuickFix, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using QuickFix you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Phone number — collected during sign-up via OTP verification (Firebase Authentication).
  • Name and email address — entered in your profile.
  • Profile photo — optional, uploaded by you.
  • Verification documents — only for service providers (e.g., ID proof) for background verification.
  • Saved addresses — for booking convenience.
  • Service request details — issue description, category, photos.
  • Before/after work photos — uploaded by service providers to verify completion.
  • Ratings, reviews, and complaints — submitted by you about services.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Precise location — used to find nearby providers and provide live tracking and ETAs. Collected only while the App is in use, and only with your permission.
  • Device identifiers and crash reports — via Firebase, to keep the App stable and secure.
  • App usage data — bookings made, screens visited, features used (anonymized).

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your contacts, SMS messages, or browsing history.
  • We do not store your full payment card details. Payments are processed by third-party providers (UPI, card networks); only transaction status is shared with us.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Verify your identity and create your account.
  • Match you with nearby service providers based on your location.
  • Enable real-time tracking, navigation, and ETAs.
  • Process bookings, payments, ratings, and complaints.
  • Send service updates and notifications (booking confirmations, status changes).
  • Improve the App's performance and detect fraudulent activity.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Your Information

We share limited information only when necessary:

  • With service providers — your name, phone number, address, and live location are shared with the provider you book, so they can reach you.
  • With customers — if you are a service provider, your name, profile photo, rating, and live location during a job are shared with the customer.
  • With third-party services — Firebase (Google) for authentication, push notifications, and analytics; payment processors for transactions.
  • For legal reasons — if required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety.

We never sell your personal data to advertisers or marketing companies.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored on secure servers and transmitted using HTTPS encryption.
  • Phone authentication is handled by Firebase, which uses industry-standard security.
  • Passwords are not stored — we use OTP-based login.
  • While we take reasonable precautions, no system is 100% secure. You use the App at your own risk.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained as long as your account is active.
  • Booking and payment history is kept for at least 3 years for legal and tax purposes.
  • You can request account deletion at any time (see Section 7).

6. Permissions We Request

  • Location (precise + approximate) — to find nearby providers and provide tracking.
  • Camera — to take profile and work-verification photos.
  • Photos / Storage — to upload images from your gallery.
  • Notifications — to send booking updates and provider arrival alerts.
  • Phone — to verify your number via OTP.

You can revoke any permission at any time in your device settings, but some features may stop working.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — view your profile, bookings, and saved info in the App.
  • Correct inaccurate information — edit your profile any time.
  • Delete your account and associated data — email us using the contact below.
  • Withdraw consent for location or notifications via device settings.
  • Object to processing or request data export.

8. Children's Privacy

QuickFix is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Third-Party Services

QuickFix uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, push notifications, analytics
  • Google Maps Platform — maps, geocoding, directions
  • Payment processors (UPI / card networks) — transaction processing

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Material changes will be communicated through the App or by email.
